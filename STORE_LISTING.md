## Full description (4000 chars max)

```
Light the athanor.
Drag the four primal elements into the Circle of Binding. Watch burning ley-lines race around the crucible. A new element rises — its true name burning across the board in gold.

Athanor: Alchemy Merge is a hand-crafted discovery game in the tradition of classic alchemy chains, themed as a dark alchemist's working circle.

▸ DISCOVER 190 ELEMENTS
Start with Water, Fire, Earth, and Air. Combine them, then combine what they produce — the chain stretches from puddles and embers all the way to gods, miracles, and the philosopher's stone. 158 hand-authored pair recipes plus 29 multi-input rituals waiting to be found.

▸ RITUAL, NOT TAPPING
Every discovery is a small ceremony. Drag two — or three, or four, or five — elements into the slots of an inscribed sigil. The crucible ignites. Channels race clockwise around the rim. The result rises in a pillar of gold, and its name burns across the board like a Ring of Power inscription.

▸ RISE THROUGH EIGHT RANKS
Initiate. Apprentice. Adept. Scholar. Mystic. Sage. Archmage. Demiurge.
Each rank shifts the sigil under your circle — from the twin lenses of the Vesica Piscis to the inscribed Triquetra, the four-fold Squaring of the Circle, and finally the five-pointed Pentacle. Wider sigils unlock deeper recipes that no two-element pair can reach.

▸ A LIVING LIBRARY
Every element you discover joins your grimoire. Filter by category, sort by recent discovery, or search by name. Tap any tile to open its Lineage — what made it, and what it makes. Open the Tree of Forging to see every discovery branching out from the four primal seeds.

▸ STUCK? BURN THE FLAME
Tap the Hint button when you're at a wall — a rewarded ad reveals the two ingredients of an undiscovered recipe whose elements you already own. The result stays a mystery. The flame keeps no count of your favors.

▸ SAVE, EXPORT, CONTINUE
Five named save slots, an automatic checkpoint, and clipboard export so you can carry your run between devices. Discoveries are forever — only Reset can erase them, and only after a double confirmation.

▸ NO ACCOUNT. NO TIMERS. NO ENERGY METERS.
You don't sign in. You don't wait. You don't grind a stamina bar. Open the app, drag two elements together, and you've started discovering. Close it, and your progress survives in your grimoire.

Built by a solo developer. Vanilla code, no engine bloat. Optimized for landscape phones — touch-friendly drag, generous tap targets, and small candlelit details everywhere you look.

A Pizza Hero Gaming project.

— Join the Discord: discord.gg/WPsNeR7wyJ
```

## Screenshot order (recommended)

When Google shows your listing, the first 3 screenshots are visible without scrolling. Put strongest first.

1. **WIND success cinematic** — the showstopper. Leads with the "I want to feel that" moment.
2. **Title screen** — brand identity. Confirms it's polished.
3. **Workspace + Library** — shows the actual gameplay.
4. **Tree of Forging** — establishes there's depth beyond the surface mechanic.
5. *(Optional)* Lineage modal — shows the recipe web.
